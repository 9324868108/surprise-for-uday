Tujhe yaad hai woh memorable moment 🤩, jab hum 11th standard mein the? Ek din Sir ne achanak se chemistry ka class test le liya tha, aur hum pure dar gaye the 😨. Sir ne 6 questions diye the, aur humne sirf 3 questions kiye the ✍, jabki poori class mein baaki logon ne sirf 1 ya 2 questions attempt kiye the. Test hone ke baad Sir ne sabke ghar call bhi kiya tha 📞, aur hamare parents se baat bhi ki thi 😭. Yaad hai? Us din puri tarah se phat chuki thi 😂. Ghar jaane ka bhi mann nahi kar raha tha, toh hum class ke bahar khade the 🤦‍♂, soch rahe the ab kya karein. Approx aadha ghanta tak khade hone ke baad 🤔, jab samajh aaya ki ab kuch nahi ho sakta 😅, tab humne ek dusre ko "All the Best" bola 🤝 aur ghar chale gaye the 🏠. Sir ke wajah se dant bhi padi thi 😵‍💫. Yaad hai na? 😂 I can't forget this moment, so that's the reason I am sharing this memory with you at this best time, buddy! 🎂🥳🎁
